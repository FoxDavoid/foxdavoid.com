The project has been started

This right here is the first post for foxdavoid.com (well, I haven't bought the domain, but that's how I called the GitHub repo like).

What is this fooor?

Well, I certainly don't know, hahaahahahahahahah. No, really, I need to think about it. This was something I thought it would be cool for my birthday. A birth-day, cuz... yeah, the webpage was born, meaning as well that this webpage might celebrate its birthday at the same time I do (or don't), lol.

So, what will this blog be about? I'm not entirely sure yet, but I envision it as a place to share my thoughts, projects, and perhaps some turorials on topics I'm passionate about. It's a space for me to document my journey and connect with others who share similar interests.

I wish this article was more like a newsletter, hahah, but this is the first one so I don't really have something to... announce besides this tiny silly project. It's just a page.

If you feel fatigued by just reading this, I'm really sorry for you, cuz this is probably one of the shortest posts ever that will be here. Like, really, I'm not kidding. You have any idea how many times I can't freely talk about literally ANYTHING? This thing might be my outlet. There are some times my thoughts get over me and I just start saying random stuff I certainly always think about ( well, not LITERALLY ALWAYS) but you get the point, I hope.

And maaybe it won't be just thoughts and thoughts one after the another, maybe... uh... well, I haven't gotten this far before when thinking about this webpage. There are some sections that you will notice perfectly at first glance, cuz they were literally on your screen before even clicking this one (or tapping? Wait, are you on phone?).

Newsletters, a cheap one, like, I don't even have a emailing service for it, hahahahaha... ha... it's because there are so many alternatives, and I haven't even bought the domain yet (I don't even know either I'll buy it or maybe I will next year as a "birthday gift" for the webpage).

Blog. You are reading one at this moment. Yeah, I said this was a newsletter, but I just found out that judging by how I am writing all this, you can perfectly tell this is not really a... newsletter, well, maybe, idk.

Feed. Here I think I will post random, quick stuff, like imagine an average person using Instagram on their daily-basis, well, I don't use Instagram, so that's up to you. Okay, no, I do have an Instagram, but to be fair, I only use it to text a friend I share things in common ( it's the one whose drawing I'm using right now as a "banner").

I'll won't explain the rest, you can literally see what it is about. Or maybe I will, but in the future, this is just a little thing I needed to write for the website to exist.
